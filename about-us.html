About Us

Seven years ago, we started making electrical plugs for homes across Texas. We assembled the plugs ourselves, purchasing the parts at Home Depot and building the enclosures.
It all started with three close friends who came together as a way to spend time with each other while helping people avoid losing electricity. Time flew by because we enjoyed what we did so much. Over time, our customers were consistently satisfied, and many began asking if we could handle other types of jobs. Eventually, we realized: why say no when we could help them solve any kind of problem? We had the solution, after all. And so, little by little, we grew. My friends and I began to take on different responsibilities within the company. Though we were no longer working side by side every day, communication remained essential to keep growing steadily. This separation allowed us to expand, but staying connected was the key to our continued progress.


At Power Legion Enterprise, we believe in:

  • Quality Service
  • Customer Satisfaction
  • Professional Excellence
  • Competitive Pricing
